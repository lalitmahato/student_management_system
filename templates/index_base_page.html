Student Management System

Our Student Management System is a web application designed to simplify academic record management for schools and institutions. The platform allows administrators to create and manage student profiles, track courses, monitor enrollments, and record performance. Instructors can be assigned to multiple courses, while metadata tagging enables flexible annotations across students, courses, and academic records.
